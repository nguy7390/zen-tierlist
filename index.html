Tier list methodology

Every patch, our experts curate a predictive tier list for climbing solo queue based recent buffs, nerfs, and trends.

We rate champions as Optimal (S-tier), Great (A-tier), or Good (B-tier) based on their ability to perform in the current meta. Champions are also given a skill floor difficulty rating to indicate how much of a time investment is needed to see positive results.

If a champion isnt on our tier list, it doesnt necessarily mean that you cant climb with them. Youll just have to put in more effort to get similar results as our Optimal, Great, and Good choices for climbing.

To learn more about our methodology, check out our blog article.

Shen has become one of the best top laners in the game that can dominate the early to mid phase of the game even as a tank. You can even opt for a more aggressive summoner spell setup that takes Ignite instead of Teleport and rushes Titanic Hydra to give you more kill pressure in lane. Shen is an amazing addition to any team as he can turn enemy ganks around and protect his teammates and make allied ganks even more potent by ulting in on his own jungler, all while being a great frontliner.

Master Yi may only be in A tier but he is very close to S. The rise of Kha'zix and Hecarim hasn't been kind but when you're able to dodge these matchups he still has one of the best late games out of any jungler. Reaching 3 items turns you Super Saiyan, ripping teams apart just by right clicking and he definitely can carry harder than most junglers.

Akali has seen a huge rise in popularity in the mid lane after her latest buffs and is pretty close to S tier. You can have little impact on her if you don't use your abilities correctly but when you get it right she can take over a teamfight completely. Having such high damage and mobility makes her a true carry in the mid lane, one that doesn't rely much on her team if at all.

With Caitlyn getting nerfs this patch, Jhin will be set to take another little rise and truly cement himself as an S tier ADC. The whole of S tier all have one thing in common, superior range. Jhin can go toe to toe with Caitlyn and Ashe, pushing the wave well and trading autos when needed but bullying other shorter range ADCs. With his crit build he is very consistent across all phases of the game, leaving almost no time when you'd wish you were playing something else.

Lux shot up in popularity during 10.16, partly due to a new bot lane combo with Sona and partly due to the fact that she had a bug with her shield that applied for more than it should. Even after these nerfs she will still be extremely powerful as a support, she does well into most meta matchups right now and has an extremely good lane phase with a lot of damage and pick potential. This is especially true when paired with something like Caitlyn, Jhin or Ezreal that are all strong now too. - Nasteey DIAMOND I
